My beginning in programming

Why I Chose KBTU

When I started thinking about universities, KBTU (Kazakh-British Technical University) quickly became my top choice. Its reputation for academic excellence, modern facilities, and the opportunity to learn from the best professors made it my dream. I also heard amazing stories from current students, which motivated me even more.The preparation process was intense and challenging. I spent countless hours studying mathematics, English, and other key subjects. Balancing schoolwork, extra preparation, and personal life was far from easy. Some days felt overwhelming, especially when I doubted whether I was doing enough. However, I kept reminding myself of my goal: getting into KBTU. To stay organized, I created a schedule and broke my studies into smaller tasks. This approach helped me focus on one step at a time. My family and friends also supported me, encouraging me when I felt like giving up.

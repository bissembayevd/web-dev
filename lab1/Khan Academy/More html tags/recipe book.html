Recipe #1

  • TPreparation: ~30 minutes
  • Cooking rice: ~20 minutes
  • Assembling sushi: ~20 minutes
  • Total: ~1 hour 10 minutes
Ingredients for rice Quantity
Sushi rice 200gr
Water 250ml
Rice vinegar 3 tbsp
Sugar 1 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Ingredients for the filling Quantity
Nori sheets 5-6 pcs
Fish (salmon, tuna) 200gr
Cucumber 1 pc
Avocado 1 pc
Shrimp 100 g
Cream cheese 100 g

Prepare the rice

Step 1: Rinse the rice under cold water several times until the water runs clear.

Step 2: Transfer the rice to a pot, add water, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 3: Remove from heat and let it rest, covered, for another 10 minutes.

Step 4: In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Heat gently until dissolved, then stir into the cooked rice. Let it cool to room temperature.

Prepare the filling

Step 1: Cut the fish into thin strips.

Step 2:Peel and slice cucumber and avocado into long strips.

Assemble the sushi

Step 1: Place a bamboo sushi mat on your work surface, covered with plastic wrap.

Step 2: Lay a sheet of nori shiny side down.

Step 3: Lay a sheet of nori shiny side down.

Step 4: Spread the rice evenly over the nori, leaving the top edge (about 2 cm) free.

Step 5: Arrange the filling (fish, cucumber, avocado, cream cheese) in the center.

Step 6: Roll up the nori tightly using the mat, pressing firmly. Wet the free edge with water to seal the roll.

Cut the sushi

Step 1:Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into 6-8 pieces. To prevent sticking, wet the knife with water before each cut.

Serve

Step 1: Arrange the sushi on a plate and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Natus Vincere (NAVI (previously the abbreviation Na`Vi[1] was used); from Latin - “Born to Win”) is a Ukrainian multi-gaming e-sports organization.

FaZe Clan (formerly FaZe Sniping) is a professional North American esports and entertainment organization headquartered in Los Angeles, United States. Founded on May 30, 2010.

Virtus pro

Virtus.pro (VP, “Bears”) is an Armenian e-sports organization founded in 2003 in Russia. The club is a seven-time champion of international Major category tournaments in Dota 2 and CS:GO, and is in the top 10 in the world in terms of prize money won (about $19.5 million)
